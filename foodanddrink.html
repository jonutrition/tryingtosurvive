Where is best to grab some grub around Hallam?

Everyone loves a good bite to eat and a drink so we have rounded up a list of some of our favourite spots!

  • Benjamin Huntsman 'spoons

    • This spot just off Division Street is great for cheap meals and 2-4-1 pitchers!

  • The canvendish on West Street

    • Lovely pub that does crazy deals like 25p chicken wings on Wednesdays!

  • The Wick at Both Ends

    • This charming little pub is always decorated with fairy lights and does delicious cocktails and pizzas.

  • The Riverside

    • Kelham Island has a great river side pub with lots of outdoor seating for you to enjoy the view. It also always plays great music.

  • The Cabin

    • If sweet treats are your thing, you'll love the pancakes and waffles that the cabin has to offer. Check out their smoothies too!

  • Couch

    • If you need somewhere to rest your legs, Couch on Ecclesall road is a great option with a very relaxed atmosphere.

    Don't forget that Sheffield Hallam University itself has loads of great places to eat within its buildings. Ranging from sandwiches and cakes at the cutting edge cafe on the 5th floor of owen building to full on burgers and beer at the hideout bar located in the student's union! Be sure to check everything out!
